# Code of Conduct

## 🤝 Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or any other dimension of diversity.

We pledge that everyone who participates in the Vercodea Auth Core community will be treated with respect and dignity, and we are dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## � Our Standards

### Examples of Behavior That Contributes to Creating a Positive Environment

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Helping others learn and grow
- Acknowledging the time and effort of contributors

### Examples of Unacceptable Behavior

- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information, such as email address or home address, without explicit permission
- Deliberate intimidation or threats
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Discrimination based on any protected characteristic
- Sharing code or documentation without proper attribution

## 🛡️ Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the maintainers:

📧 **Email:** vercodea@gmail.com

**Please include:**
- Description of the violation
- Who was involved (if safe to share)
- When and where it occurred
- Any relevant context or evidence
- Your preferred contact method for follow-up

All reports will be handled confidentially. We will not disclose the identity of the reporter without their consent.

### Investigation & Response

1. **24 hours** - We acknowledge receipt of your report
2. **7 days** - We investigate and determine appropriate action
3. **14 days** - We communicate our decision and next steps
4. **30 days** - We implement corrective measures if needed

### Possible Outcomes

Depending on the severity and nature of the violation:

| Violation | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| **Minor incident** | Private warning, discussion of expectations |
| **Moderate incident** | Public apology required, temporary mute/restrictions |
| **Serious incident** | Suspension from community (temporary or permanent) |
| **Severe violation** | Permanent ban from project and platforms |

## 🌍 Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project repositories and source code
- Issue trackers and pull requests
- Discussions and forums
- Chat channels (Discord, Slack, etc.)
- Social media posts related to the project
- Community events and conferences
- Any official project communication

This Code of Conduct applies both within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community.

## 🤲 Community Responsibilities

### As a Community Member

- Treat all members with respect and kindness
- Challenge ideas, not people
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Respect privacy and confidentiality
- Help create an inclusive environment
- Report violations promptly and constructively

### As a Maintainer

- Set the example through your own behavior
- Enforce this Code of Conduct fairly and consistently
- Take all reports seriously and investigate promptly
- Protect the reporter's privacy
- Work to resolve conflicts constructively
- Support members in understanding and improving behavior

## ✅ Commitment to Improvement

Everyone makes mistakes. We believe in:

- **Growth** - Giving people room to learn and improve
- **Accountability** - Taking responsibility for our actions
- **Redemption** - Believing people can change
- **Restorative justice** - Focusing on repair rather than punishment alone

If you have violated this Code of Conduct, we encourage you to:

1. Acknowledge the impact of your actions
2. Take steps to make amends
3. Demonstrate genuine change over time
4. Seek to understand different perspectives
